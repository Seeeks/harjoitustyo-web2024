How to play

Click or tap to move the hero in the direction of the click. Gather apples while avoiding cacti.

After every 3 apples have been harvested, 3 new apples will appear, as well as 2 stationary cacti.

When the first 2 apples have been harvested, and for every 5 apples after, a moving bigger cactus will appear.
